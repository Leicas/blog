Mathematical Syntax

Below is the mathematical typography that is used on this site.

A non-bold standard letter is used to denote a scalar:

$$ c = 3e8 $$

An arrow is used to denote a vector:

$$ \vec{v} = \begin{bmatrix}1\\2\\3\end{bmatrix} $$

A hat is used to denote a unit vector:

$$ \hat{x} = \begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{bmatrix} $$

A bold capital letter is used to denote a matrix:

$$ \mathbf{B} = \begin{bmatrix}1&2&3\\4&5&6\\7&8&9\end{bmatrix} $$

A bold lower-case letter is used to denote the imaginary component (in things such as complex numbers and quaternions).

$$ c = a + b\mathbf{i} $$

$$ q = a + b\mathbf{i} + c\mathbf{j} + d\mathbf{k} $$
