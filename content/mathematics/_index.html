Mathematical Syntax

A non-bold standard letter is used to denote a scalar:

$$ c = 3e8 $$

A hat is used to denote a vector:

$$ \hat{v} = \begin{bmatrix}1\\2\\3\end{bmatrix} $$

A bold capital letter is used to denote a matrix:

$$ \matrix{B} = \begin{bmatrix}1&2&3\\4&5&6\\7&8&9\end{bmatrix} $$
