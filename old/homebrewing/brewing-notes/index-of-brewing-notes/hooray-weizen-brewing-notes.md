# Ingredients


Malt



	  * 2kg Wheat
	  * 1kg Weyermann Pilsner
	  * 0.5kg Vienna



## Hops





	  * 10g Hallertauer 7.5%AA (pellets)



## Yeast








	  * Safbrew Wheat Yeast WB-06






## Other





	  * Zest of 5 Lemons
	  * 2x 500g Budget Honey
